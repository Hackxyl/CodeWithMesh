❓ FAQ

Questions? I've got answers 😊

Everything you need to know before signing up. Still unsure? Just message me!

Do I need any prior coding experience?▼
Absolutely not! Many of my students start with zero experience. I have beginner-friendly courses designed for people who have never written a single line of code. We start from scratch and build up together at your pace. 😊
How do the live sessions work?▼
After you enroll, we schedule your first session via Google Meet or Zoom. I share my screen, walk you through lessons live, and you can ask questions at any time. Sessions are recorded so you can rewatch them. We typically do 2–3 sessions per week depending on your availability.
How long does it take to complete a course?▼
Most students complete a course in 6–12 weeks doing 2–3 sessions per week. There's no rush — we go at your pace. You'll have lifetime access to all materials even after the course ends.
What payment methods do you accept?▼
We accept M-PESA, credit/debit cards (Visa & Mastercard), and bank transfers. You can also pay in installments — just reach out and we'll work something out. All prices are in Kenyan Shillings (KES).
What if I'm not happy with the course?▼
I offer a full 7-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If you attend your first session and feel it's not the right fit, let me know within 7 days and I'll refund you completely. Your satisfaction matters most. 🛡️
Will I get a certificate when I finish?▼
Yes! Every student who completes all lessons and the final project receives a personalized certificate of completion. You can add this to your LinkedIn, CV, or portfolio to show employers your new skills. 🏆
Can I contact you between sessions?▼
Yes! All students get a direct WhatsApp/chat line for questions between sessions. Stuck on something? Send a message and I'll respond — usually within a few hours during the day. 💬

Still have questions? Feel free to reach out — or just jump in! 🚀
